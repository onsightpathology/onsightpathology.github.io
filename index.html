Bridging the Gap in Digital Pathology

Precise microscopic examination is paramount for disease diagnosis, yet it's often prone to subjective variability. While artificial intelligence shows immense promise, real-world deployment is hindered by proprietary software, complex integrations, and data privacy concerns.

OnSight is the solution. It's a freely accessible, standalone software that provides real-time AI inferences directly on your screen as you work. By analyzing a region of your display, OnSight bypasses WSI format incompatibilities and the need for complex APIs, allowing for safe, cost-effective integration into any existing research or clinical workflow.

Proven Applications

OnSight comes with a suite of pre-trained models to assist with common histopathological tasks. Users can easily switch between models to fit their needs.

Brain Tumor Classification

Utilizes a powerful Vision Transformer (ViT) to provide real-time classification of common brain tumor patterns (glial, meningiothelial, epithelial, schwannian) on H&E-stained slides, guiding initial diagnosis.

Mitosis Detection & Quantification

Employs a RetinaNet object detection model to automatically identify and highlight mitotic figures. Users can adjust the confidence threshold in real-time to balance sensitivity and specificity for accurate tumor grading.

Immunohistochemistry (IHC) Quantification

A YOLO-based model segments and counts Ki-67 positive and negative nuclei, providing a rapid and objective proliferation index. Includes controls for overlay transparency to aid interpretation.

Interactive AI Chat

Integrates a Vision-Language Model (HuaTuo) that supports natural-language queries about the current image. Ask questions like, "What feature makes this glial?" to get deeper, interpretable insights from the AI.
